Wishing you joy, love & peace

Our 2025 was one of change and one of loss, but there were plenty of celebrations, too! We gained two loved ones to our state, we mourned our family pet and saw Presley become a bride, not one but two. We were joined this past spring by Kristi’s parents, Jerry and Linda— from shores of the Big Island of Hawaii to an ethereal new home in Laguna Woods. Staying busy with hiking, gardening, and estate sale hunting, it looks like the fit is real good! Many of you have seen our pictures of our beloved dog, Rocko. Our good boy was needed in heaven this year after 14 years of guarding our home from sketchy mailmen and squirrels. He was given many treats in his lifetime, but his dad would give him treats from the dinner table, which he knew would lead to quarrels. (And it was worth it!) We were fortunate to enjoy a week in the state we grew up in—Colorado. It was a combination of walking down memory lane, visiting family, and seeing our niece Presley get hitched. Let’s spend 2026 lifting each other up. Pray for each other, be kind, be forgiving and polite. “One Love, One Heart— let’s get together and feel alright!”
